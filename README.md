## About Me

I am a **UI/UX Designer and Web Designer** based in Constanța, Romania, passionate about creating digital experiences that feel intuitive, purposeful, and visually refined.

My approach combines user-centered thinking, strong visual hierarchy, and responsive design. I enjoy transforming ideas into polished interfaces—from early structure and wireframes to interactive prototypes and web-ready experiences.

- Focused on clean, accessible, and intuitive interfaces
- Interested in design systems and consistent product experiences
- Comfortable moving from concept and research to high-fidelity design
- Attentive to both visual detail and real user needs

> Open to freelance projects, creative collaborations, and opportunities in UI/UX and web design.
